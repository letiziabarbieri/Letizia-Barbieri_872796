Interior Design

Re-Collection

For this lab critical thinking and concept
generation were a primary focus, where drawing
and model making activities were integrated
to establish this process. The primary concern
of the course was to foster strategies for
dealing with product form, function and use
via observation techniques. Then we had to apply
these techniques to the solution of a design
problem and its resolution through a process
of design. The theme of that year was the
revaluation of a disused space in a rough
district of Milan. The project of my group was
to turn it into a sheltered place, where
people of all ages could gather and spend time
together. Recollection was a museum of the
people, where everyone could bring something
that belonged to them and stood for them.
It turned out to be more of an experiential
process, through which people became acquainted
to other people by getting to know themselves
first, tearing down social barriers and prejudices.

Appartamento Bolchini

The aim of the course was testing technical
multimedia skills in the contest of communication
within industrial design. Therefore, I was trained
to design and realize the presentation of a project
by applying the acquired know-how about multiple
media tools. These techical boards would represent
the revaluation of Apartment Bolchini, a loft
designed in the early thirties by Luciano Baldessari.

The fifth

This lab introduced and developed the role of
visual languages and of their respective tools
and techniques aimed at the morphological,
material and functional representation of the
project, through the development and interpretation
of perceptive mechanisms and chromatic systems.
My group designed the visual communication around
The Fifth, a speakeasy hypothetically set in New
York. The installation that is showed in the
pictures sums up the projects we delivered
throughout the semester.
